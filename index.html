Fooder

The food scanning app

Fooder is an app where the user can scan foods s/he has at home to get different recipes. The idea behind the app is to not throw away so much food that get’s left over in our homes. If you have a package of eggs and some potatoes left, you can scan the them and get a great recipe of a Spanish potato omelette.

Groceries

The Smart Shopping List

I decided to create this app because I wanted an app to help me organize my grocery shopping better so that similar products which are located in the same place in the store are beside each other in the list, so that you don’t have to run back and forth in the store that much anymore.

Passenger

The car sharing app

This app is a car sharing app where the driver can save money by picking up passengers who are going in the same direction as the driver. By picking up more passengers the driver earns points which he then can use to get a free car wash or a check up.
